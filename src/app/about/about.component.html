About Scholance

End the experience gap with open educational freelancing

There's a big problem when an "entry-level" job requires 3 years prior experience. Scholance is here to fix that.

Scholance allows businesses, non-profits, and governmental organizations to post real-world projects for students to be able to work on for their portfolios. When a project is complete, the business can select the best of all the entries and use it. And regardless of whether a student is selected or not, they can still use the completed work for their portfolio.

Non-zero-sum mentality

Internships and traditional freelancing pose a significant barrier of entry to anyone getting started business. Students and recent graduates are assumed to have a face-value of zero or close to it. They are still forced though to take such deflated opportunities in order to build enough experience to prove their value.
