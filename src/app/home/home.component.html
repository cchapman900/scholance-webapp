Open Educational Freelancing

For Students

Open access to work any project. Use any work you do for your portfolio.

  • No barrier-of-entry

    Scholance breaks down a significant barrier-of-entry to getting access to meaningful experience. Rather than committing yourself to a months-long unpaid internship, or a menial pre-entry-level job, or the grueling process of becoming an established freelancer, Scholance lets you find the types of projects your interested in and get started working on them right away. Build your portfolio with projects that are right for you.

  • Make things. Not lattes.

    Wherever you are in your education, Scholance lets you get started immediately on the types of projects that are right for you. There is no barrier of entry to getting started on projects, and whatever work you do is yours to use for your portfolio.

For Businesses & Organizations

Post projects for students to work on. Select the best of the submissions for your business use.

  • Skip the commitment issues

    Whether you're a one-person startup, non-profit or leading a Fortune 500, there's always more work to be done than resources available allow. See what a pool of capable and enthusiastic candidates can accomplish that a single entity may not. Rather than committing to the singularity of a traditional freelancer or the daunting costs of professional consultants, Scholance gives you access to students from around the world to work simultaneously on getting work done for your organization. Commit to a result only when it is there and ready to go.

  • Tap into all of tomorrow's potential

    Channel the creativity, fresh perspective, and dedication of students around the world for your organization's needs.
